Key Features

  • Dynamic Gradient: Extracts dominant color from playlist cover (2x2 mosaic) and creates vertical gradient fading to #121212
  • 2x2 Mosaic Cover: Grid of 4 album art thumbnails (~110px each) forming a square collage
  • Action Bar: Play (green circle), Shuffle, Download - left-aligned only
  • Song List: Table with #, Title (with album thumb), Album, Date Added, Duration
  • Hover States: Track hover shows lighter background, track number becomes play icon
